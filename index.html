About/Bio

Welcome to my homepage!

I am currently a Research Scientist in Machine Learning at Meta Platforms Inc. (parent co. of Facebook), working in the Probability team.

I graduated with a PhD in Dec 2021 from the University of Washington (Seattle), contributing research at the intersection of Machine/Reinforcement Learning and Computational Neuroscience.

Prior to this, I earned an MS in Computer Science at Oregon State University, and an MS in Electrical & Computer Engineering at the University of Iowa, and also worked in industry for ~5 years:

  • Data Scientist on the LinkedIn.com Data Science team
  • ML/Data Engineer and Product Manager at Orbitz.com (now Expedia)
  • (Chip) Design Engineer at Texas Instruments
  • Robotics Intern at Systemantics
